Endless Summer
Surf School

We cultivate a love and respect for the ocean by building confidence, developing character and inspiring stoke through safety, fun and learning.

Endless Summer Surf School offers programs to complete beginners, intermediate and advanced surfers of any age.

Come on a learn to surf journey of a lifetime in Manu Bay as we set off on a half day adventure. Sit back and relax after we pick you up from your accommodation and begin the search.

With each lesson we cover ocean safety. This ensures at the end of your experience you have knowledge to know where is a safe area to surf or swim for the future

We have over 80 fun safe soft surfboards and intermediate to advanced boards that will help you to learn faster and catch lots of waves.

We are the only Manu Bay surf school to have surf school permits to teach personal private lessons & group lessons in both Manu & Whale Bay.

Our lessons are specifically designed to teach you to surf at your own pace in a relaxed environment to build further self-confidence so you can continue in the sport of surfing and feel confident in the water.

Instructors

All of our surf coaches hold a current Surfing New Zealand coaching accreditation and APOLA Beach lifeguard certification.

Our team of instructors are career surf guides with a minimum of 10 years coaching background and 15 years personal surfing experience both in New Zealand and overseas

All instructors have vast knowledge of the local area and will only conduct lessons in surf conditions in waist deep water with easy spilling broken waves perfect for progressing

As a mobile surf school we are dedicated to finding the best, fun and safest waves daily. We have over 10 different locations at our fingertips, ready for the ultimate learn to surf experience. Our beaches are the best in New Zealand, you will learn in waist deep water with easy to ride small waves.
